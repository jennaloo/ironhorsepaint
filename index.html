About Me

I am Tim Murphy, Professional Airbrush Artist. I have been an artist for over 30 years. I learned to airbrush while working for American Ironhorse Motorcycles of Fort Worth, TX, a manufacturer of high quality, custom choppers. My previous art experience combined with the training received at Ironhorse on the use of an airbrush brought me to a skill level where I can now create some of the most intricate, highly detailed art for motorcycles, cars, trucks and more.
